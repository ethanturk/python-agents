Integrated Pest Management (IPM) strategies are often employed to minimize chemical usage while maximizing yield. Water management is another critical factor; the spinach is sensitive to both drought and waterlogging. The growing cycle varies by variety, but most commercial spinach crops are harvested within a specific window to ensure peak flavor and texture.
